Why DCAMP

Built like a product. Delivered like a car. Galvanized steel structure. Fluorocarbon aluminum cladding. Acoustic glazing. Whole-house intelligence — out of the box.

Factory-Built Envelope

Galvanized steel frame, fluorocarbon aluminum cladding, insulated and waterproofed structure. Acoustic-rated insulated glazing. Laminated insulated skylight. Painted stainless entry door. Built to outlast a typical site-built home — without the site-built headaches.

Engineering

Move-in Ready Bundle

Comfort mattress, mattress protector, duvet, pillows, sheets, towels, bathrobe, kettle, mugs, hangers, hair dryer, slippers, umbrellas, custom amenities. Eighty-plus items pre-installed. Open the door, the lights are already on.

Inventory

Smart-Home Native

Whole-house smart control with voice activation. Hotel-grade smart lock. Speaker, climate and appliance scene panels. Motorized curtains and skylight blackout blinds. Fresh-air system, central A/C, underfloor heating — orchestrated, not bolted on.
